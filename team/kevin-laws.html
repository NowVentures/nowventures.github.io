Kevin Laws Investment Committee

Kevin Laws is an active angel investor, COO at AngelList, and partner at Maiden Lane Ventures. Previously, he was founder & CEO of Vast.com, driving deals with Yahoo, AOL, and Microsoft/Bing to power portions of their search technology. He was a principal at PacRim Venture Partners, VP Product at Epinions, a manager in the Media Group at Booz & Co, and headed software development for CSC’s Heidelberg, Germany office. He holds a degree in computer science from Dickinson College, a master’s in International Relations from Boston University, and an MBA from the MIT Sloan School of Management. Kevin serves on the Investment Committee of NOW.
