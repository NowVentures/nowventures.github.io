NOW is the world’s first funding platform dedicated to enabling early-stage companies with transformative solutions for a prosperous world. Companies that exist to make the world better will be positioned to capture larger market share, create loyal customers, attract and retain talent. Their financial success means everyone benefits. We exist to help these startups raise capital and succeed.
