NOW is the first platform built to enable early-stage companies with transformative solutions for a prosperous world. NOW invests seed capital in startups with disruptive ideas. Our founders are passionate, mission-driven and poised to create companies that outperform.

Financial returns and positive social good, are not mutually exclusive, but mutually enhancing. Companies that exist to make the world better are positioned to capture larger market share, create loyal customers, attract and retain talent. Their financial success means everyone benefits.
